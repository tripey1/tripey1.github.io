Our Story

Tripey is a mobile application that help travelers explore Bangladesh with bringing all the services to their hand.

All you need to do is tap and go.

We focused on prioritizing the basic needs of a traveler and make their life easier and simple.

Before leaving for your trip, you may arrange everything from your bus tickets to the tour guide you'll need on your trip using smartphone.

Our Services

We offer a wide variety of services for you to choose from, depending on your dream destination!

From bus to train, hop into your preferred mode to travel.

Get accommodated in a mud-house or be it in a luxury hotel, we got you covered.

Involved with the community - get the best of your trip while empowering local community

Check & choose from our smart geo-located mapping - To indulge into mouthwatering local food on-the-go.
